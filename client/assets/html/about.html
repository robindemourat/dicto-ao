Dicto is a tool aimed at improving the usage of interviews or conferences footages in journalistic and academic contexts.

It is aimed at supporting three types of activities :

  • Interview transcription/subtitling and timecodes refinement through a unique visual and keyboard-friendly interface.
  • Interview coding and tagging, and export in table and text format for further analysis.
  • Interviews reading/watching, with the possibility of defining "montages" inside a media piece, that is a subset of relevant moments and share them as reworked/remixed pieces of media.

Roadmap for further development :

  • Integration of youtube and soundrop as possible media inputs
  • Support for .txt files and fuzzy recognition of non-orthodox transcription's timecoding and formatting
